

## Countdown Launch Application

A bold and dramatic countdown application with a custom backdrop image that launches to a destination site after 5 seconds.

### Features

**1. Full-Screen Backdrop Image**
- Upload your own image from your device to use as the background
- The image will cover the entire screen with a dark overlay for readability
- Option to remove or change the image anytime

**2. Launch Button**
- Prominent "Launch" button in the center of the screen
- Eye-catching design that stands out against the backdrop
- Hover effects for interactivity

**3. 5-Second Countdown Display**
- Large, bold countdown numbers (5, 4, 3, 2, 1)
- Dramatic animations as each number appears (scale/pulse effects)
- Smooth transitions between numbers
- Final "LAUNCH!" message before redirect

**4. Automatic Redirect**
- After countdown completes, automatically opens the destination site
- Placeholder URL included (easy to change later)

### Visual Design
- Bold typography for maximum impact
- Dramatic scale and pulse animations on countdown numbers
- Dark overlay on backdrop image for contrast
- Modern, clean button styling with hover effects
